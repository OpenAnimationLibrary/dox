Detailed Description

A dock placeholder contains the necessary information about a possible docking solution.

Dock placeholders are top-level widgets used by a DockLayout to draw docking solutions for a dragged DockWidget. They are actually generated when dragging of a DockWidget begins: if it belongs to a parent DockLayout, docking possibilities are calculated and stored into the layout. Placeholders selection and activation depend on the dragged dock window and therefore are of its own responsibility. You may, however, inherit this class to provide custom placeholders; in this case, a DockDecoAllocator class reimplementing allocation of placeholders must be assigned to the owner dock widget.
