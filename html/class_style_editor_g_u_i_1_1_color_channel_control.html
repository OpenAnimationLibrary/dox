Detailed Description

ColorChannelControl is the widget used to show/edit a channel. 

        Inherits \b QWidget.

        The ColorChannelControl is composed of three object: a label \b

QLabel to show the channel name, and an IntLineEdit and a ColorSlider to show/edit the channel value.
