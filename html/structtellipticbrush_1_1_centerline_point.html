Detailed Description

CenterlinePoint contains the data a about a discretized centerline stroke point - which includes its position, and eventual forward and backward derivative-like directions. Thickness data is included in the structure.

This information is necessary and sufficient to build associated outline points, and eventual additional points related to caps.
