enum PaletteViewerGUI::PaletteViewType

Possible palette contents of a Palette Viewer.

Useful to mark different palette viewer type.

There are three possible palette to view:

  • level palette, palette of current level;
  • cleanup palette, current scene cleanup palette;
  • studio palette.
