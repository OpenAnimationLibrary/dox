\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{structtellipticbrush_1_1_centerline_point}{Centerline\+Point}} contains the data a about a discretized centerline stroke point -\/ which includes its position, and eventual forward and backward derivative-\/like directions. Thickness data is included in the structure.

This information is necessary and sufficient to build associated outline points, and eventual additional points related to caps.
