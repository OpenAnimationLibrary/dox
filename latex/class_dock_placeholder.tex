\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A dock placeholder contains the necessary information about a possible docking solution.

Dock placeholders are top-\/level widgets used by a \mbox{\hyperlink{class_dock_layout}{Dock\+Layout}} to draw docking solutions for a dragged \mbox{\hyperlink{class_dock_widget}{Dock\+Widget}}. They are actually generated when dragging of a \mbox{\hyperlink{class_dock_widget}{Dock\+Widget}} begins\+: if it belongs to a parent \mbox{\hyperlink{class_dock_layout}{Dock\+Layout}}, docking possibilities are calculated and stored into the layout. Placeholders selection and activation depend on the dragged dock window and therefore are of its own responsibility. You may, however, inherit this class to provide custom placeholders; in this case, a \mbox{\hyperlink{class_dock_deco_allocator}{Dock\+Deco\+Allocator}} class reimplementing allocation of placeholders must be assigned to the owner dock widget.
