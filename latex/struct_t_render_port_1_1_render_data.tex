\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This storage struct contains the elementary data necessary to identify a cluster of identical rendered frames from \mbox{\hyperlink{class_t_renderer}{TRenderer}}. In order to avoid recalculation of identical frames during a render process, they are merged in one \textquotesingle{}equivalence cluster\textquotesingle{} of which only one representant is rendered; the m\+\_\+frames member stores each frame of such cluster. The associated raster is stored in the m\+\_\+ras member. Additional returned infos include the \mbox{\hyperlink{class_t_render_settings}{TRender\+Settings}} under which the frames were rendered, and unique identification vars.
