\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The keyframe of a plastic skeleton vertex deformation. 

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
A deformation keyframe stores vertex deformation keyframes by vertex {\itshape names}. This is the approach we use to deal with keyframe pasting to different skeletons. 
\end{DoxyNote}
