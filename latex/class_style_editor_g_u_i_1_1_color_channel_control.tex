\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{class_style_editor_g_u_i_1_1_color_channel_control}{Color\+Channel\+Control}} is the widget used to show/edit a channel. 

\begin{DoxyVerb}        Inherits \b QWidget.

        The ColorChannelControl is composed of three object: a label \b
\end{DoxyVerb}
 QLabel to show the channel name, and an {\bfseries{Int\+Line\+Edit}} and a \mbox{\hyperlink{class_style_editor_g_u_i_1_1_color_slider}{Color\+Slider}} to show/edit the channel value.
